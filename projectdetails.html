Smart Craddle using IOT

  • Developed a smart craddle using IOT.
  • This system can detect the emotions of the baby cry or laugh.

  • Captured images will be sent to the parents directly over IOT server.
  • Toy and fan can be controlled.
  • Temperature and humidity can be measured and can be monitored over IOT.
  • Cradle can be oscillated with the help of Blynk App.
